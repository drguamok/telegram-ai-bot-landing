FAQ

Are my messages secure?

Yes. All messages are encrypted with a strong algorithm. We cannot read your chats — even if we wanted to.

What does "remembers" mean?

The bot saves important facts you tell it: name, preferences, plans. In the next conversation, it already knows what you talked about before.

Is it free?

Yes, there's a free tier with a 50 messages per day limit. For active use, there are paid plans without limits.

How do I delete all data?

Send the /delete command in the bot chat — and all your data will be permanently erased.

Does it work in other languages?

Yes, the bot understands and responds in many languages. It automatically detects the language of your messages.
