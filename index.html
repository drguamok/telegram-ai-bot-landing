FAQ

Is my data safe?

Yes. All messages are encrypted with AES-256. We use zero-knowledge architecture — even we can't read your conversations.

What models do you use?

We use state-of-the-art models from OpenAI and Anthropic. With BYOK mode, you choose exactly which model and provider to use.

What does "10-day memory" mean?

The bot creates daily summaries of conversations and maintains rolling context. It remembers key facts and topics for up to 10 days.

Can I self-host this?

Yes. We provide a Docker image and setup guide. Self-hosted users get the same features with complete data sovereignty.

How do I cancel?

Send /cancel to the bot or manage in settings. No questions asked, instant cancellation.
